Rules:

1. Players will take turns placing pegs onto the board
2. Pegs that are knight's position away from each other will form a connection
3. Connections that are intercepted by an opponent's connection will not form
4. Players are not allowed to place a peg onto the opponents edges
5. First player to be able to form a connection from edge to edge wins the game

How to Play:

1. Select a hole that does not contain a peg to place your peg
2. Player's turn will end when a peg is placed and other player's turn will begin
